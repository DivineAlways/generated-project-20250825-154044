Wireframe Notes

Overall: Single-page layout for a clear, linear user journey.

Nav: Sticky navigation for easy access to sections. "Enroll" is highlighted.

Hero: Must grab attention immediately. Headline is the most critical copy. CTA should be above the fold.

Benefits: Use icons to make benefits easily scannable.

Modules: Accordion UI to keep the section clean and prevent overwhelming the user. Clicking expands to show details.

Testimonials: Social proof is key for trust. Include names and handles if possible.

Enrollment: Simple, clear pricing. No hidden fees. CTA button should be high-contrast and unmissable.

Footer: Standard legal and contact information.
